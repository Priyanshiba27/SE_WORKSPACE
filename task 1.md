***(A) Name 5 IT job roles and write one line about what each role does.***



Here are 5 common IT job roles and what they do:



1. **Software Developer** – Designs, writes, tests, and maintains computer programs and applications.



2\. **Network Administrator** – Manages and maintains an organization's computer networks and internet connections.



3\. **Database Administrator (DBA)** – Organizes, secures, and manages databases to ensure data is available and accurate.



4\. **Cybersecurity Analyst** – Protects computer systems and networks from cyber threats, attacks, and unauthorized access.



5\. **IT Support Specialist** – Helps users troubleshoot and resolve hardware, software, and technical issues.

***(E) What is the difference between a developer and a tester?***



**Developer**: A developer designs, writes, and maintains software code to create applications, features, and systems according to user requirements.



**Tester**: A tester evaluates the software by finding bugs, checking functionality, and ensuring the application meets quality standards before it is released to users.
